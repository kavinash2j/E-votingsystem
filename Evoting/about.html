About e-Vote

An online e-voting system is a digital platform that allows voters to cast their ballots securely and conveniently over the internet. It offers features like voter authentication, privacy, and auditability to ensure a transparent and reliable election process. Key benefits include:
Convenience: Voters can participate from anywhere using a computer or mobile device.
Security: Advanced encryption ensures votes are secure and anonymous.
Transparency: The system provides audit trails to verify the integrity of results.
Efficiency: Faster vote counting and result declaration compared to traditional methods.
Online e-voting is increasingly used in organizations, educational institutions, and some governmental elections to streamline and modernize the voting process.
